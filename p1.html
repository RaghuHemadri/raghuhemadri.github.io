Environment Adaptive Self multi task learning Navigation Robot

  • Made a robot using generalization of the reinforcement learning framework that combines flexible multitask learning, off-policy training, and the ability to learn directly from real-world events that can be detected automatically
  • Simulated RC and a real-world RC car can gather data and train fully autonomously without any human provided labels beyond those needed to train the detectors, and then at test-time be able to accomplish a variety of different tasks(In process of building real-world RC)
