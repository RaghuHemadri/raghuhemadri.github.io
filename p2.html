Non-Convex approaches to RPCA and applications to Computer Vision problems

  • The method we developed transcend the problem of low-rank and sparse matrix decomposition and applicable to solve inverse problems often encountered in several areas. We developed an efficient alternative representation corresponding to the mini max-concave penalty (MCP): re-weighed MCP (RMCP). Also developed re-weighted 𝛾 –penalty (RGP), which is the RMCP defined over the singular values of a matrix, as a non convex relaxation to the matrix rank
  • This work out performs current SOTA which employs a heuristic weight update, and is submitted to IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence
